Agency

An Agency Coordinator is a professional who typically works in advertising, marketing, or public relations agencies. Their primary responsibility is to manage and coordinate various tasks and activities within the agency to ensure projects are completed successfully and on time.

List of three physical/personality traits

  1. The job often involves juggling multiple projects with tight deadlines
  2. Meeting project deadlines and managing unexpected issues may require working long hours
  3. Dealing with demanding clients or difficult personalities can be challenging and stressful.
